Malala Yousafzai

  • Full Name: Malala Yousafzai
  • Birthdate: 12th July, 1997

Malala Yousafzai was born in Mingora, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. She was educated mostly by her father, who is a poet, school owner, and educational activist. He would allow her to stay up at night and discuss politics after her two brothers had been sent to bed.

From as young an age as eleven, Yousafzai started speaking about educational rights. In 2009, she began as a trainee then peer educator in the Open Minds Pakistan youth programme.
