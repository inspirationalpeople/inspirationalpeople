J K Rowling

  • Full Name: Joanne Rowling
  • Birthdate: 31st July, 1965

J K Rowling was born in Gloucestershire, where she spend her childhood. Many of her life experiences in her childhood and early adulthood were reflected in the Harry Potter books.

J K Rowling has always said that the premise for Harry Potter came into her head "fully-formed". Although the initial Harry Potter manuscripts were rejected by 12 publishers, "Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows" broke the record for the fastest selling book of all time, with 11 million copies sold in the first day.
