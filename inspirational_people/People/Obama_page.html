Barack Obama

Full Name: Barack Hussein Obama II Birthdate: 4th August, 1961

Barack Obama was born in Honolulu, Hawaii. He spent most of his childhood in Hawaii, but also spent a year in Washington and four years in Indonesia. In 1988 Obama enrolled in Havard Law School, and after graduating he became a civil rights attorney and professor, and taught constitutional law at the University of Chicago Law School from 1992 to 2004

Obama's primary legacy is considered to be the Affordable Care Act, aka "Obamacare". He also advanced LGBT rights, for example signing the "Don't Ask, Don't Tell Repeal Act", which banned open sevice from LBG people. A 2017 C-SPAN Presidential Historians Survey ranked Obama as the 12th best US president.
