Katherine Johnson

Full Name: Katherine Goble Johnson Birthdate: September 26, 1910

Katherine Johnson grew up in a household that valued the importance of education. Johnson gradutaed from West Viginia State with a degree in Math Education and French.

Her calculations helped to synchronize Project Apollo's Lunar Module with the moon-orbiting Apollo Command Service Module, and she and Al Hamer collaborated on backup calculations that played a role in the safe return of astronauts in the Apollo 13 mission (see An approach guidance method using a single onboard optical measurement). She also worked on the Space Shuttle.
