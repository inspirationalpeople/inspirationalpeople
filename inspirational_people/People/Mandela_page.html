Nelson Mandela

  • Full Name: Nelson Rolihlahla Mandela
  • Birthdate: 18th July, 1918

Nelson Mandela was a South African anti-apartheid revolutionary, who became South Africa's first black head of state, and teh first elected in a fully representative democratic election. His primary focus while in government was dismantling the institutional racism of apartheid and fostering racial reconciliation.

He was born in the village of Mvezo in Umtata, and was given the forename Rolihlahla, which means "troublemaker". His great-grandfather was the king of the Thembu people, and although Mandela was inelegible to inherit the throne, his early life was dominated by traditional Thembu custom and taboo.

He studied law at the University of Witwatersrand, where he was teh only black African student. It was here that he was among a delegation to start a youth wing to mobilise Africans in opposition to their subjegation, the first step in his political career.
